# CareerLens AI

> **AI-Powered Resume Screening & Intelligent Recruitment Platform**

CareerLens AI is an AI-powered recruitment platform designed to streamline the hiring process for both job seekers and recruiters. The system leverages Artificial Intelligence (AI), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to analyze resumes, evaluate ATS compatibility, match candidates with job descriptions, rank applicants based on qualifications, and provide actionable resume improvement recommendations.

---

# Overview

Traditional recruitment involves manually reviewing hundreds of resumes, making it time-consuming and prone to human bias. CareerLens AI automates resume screening and candidate evaluation, enabling recruiters to identify top talent quickly while helping applicants optimize their resumes for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).
