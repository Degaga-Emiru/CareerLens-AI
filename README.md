# CareerLens-AI
AI-Powered Resume Screening & Recruitment Platform
CareerLens AI is an intelligent recruitment platform that helps job seekers optimize their resumes and enables recruiters to identify the best candidates quickly. The platform leverages Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and OCR to analyze resumes, calculate ATS compatibility, match candidates with job descriptions, and rank applicants based on their qualifications. �
